Youth Parliament Registration

The Parliament is the legislative organ of the Union Government. It occupies a pre-eminent and central position in Indian democratic political system. Youth Parliament is one such attempt by Literary Society to bring up the ambience of the Parliamentary form of government also known as “Westminster” model of government.RULES:
1. Team size is 2.
2. The session of parliament will take place for 2 days.
3. Participants will be provided with their respective portfolios.
4. The questions for the question hour will be in accordance with your portfolios.
5. Any request for the change of portfolios shall not be entertained.
6. Day1:After the opening speech of the honourable President, the speaker will continue with the proceedings of the house. The proceedings of day 1 will be as follows : Question hour,Debate,Discussion.
7. Day 2 : Day 2 starts with the discussion on the crisis and a final report should be submitted by the members.The house will continue with another discussion.The proceedings will end with a bill passed by the house after amendments made by members and ractified by the honourable speaker.
8. THE HOUSE WILL STAND ADJOURNED SINE DIE.
9. Honourable members are advised to practice parliamentary politics with immense patience, decency and courtesy.
The organising team has all the rights to make any changes in the above mentioned rules.

Events

Panchayati Raj

We all have read the story Panch Parmeshwar, which reigns right form being on the one side to the other side of justice, and this event is presented here for you to speak for your cause before the case is closed. you can bring in your jurisprudence and your Turban laughs.Rules for the event as such:
1)No obscene language is allowed
2)debate in Regional language is allowed as in ( Marwadi, bhojpuri, rajasthani)
3)you have to report half an hour before the event starts.
4) time limit and other details will be provided on spot.
5) team size is 5.


Literati Combat

Combatting for the ultimate crown with your wit has always been one of the greatest forms of mind sports. Literati Combat is one such awe-inspiring event that is going to tickle your gray cells and test your literary knowledge. Are you ready to put your literary knowledge to the ultimate test? Then come join us to emerge as the true winner.Rules:
1) Team size is 2.
2) The event will be conducted in two Rounds.
Round 1:A quiz based on Mythical creatures. The participants need to identify the characters based on the description given. Passing is allowed.
Round 2:An extempore round in which one member of a team have to weave and recite a story based on the condition and situation given. They'll be judged on creativity and recitation.
3) In case of any discrepancies, the decision of quiz masters shall be final.


SPEAKER'S DILEMMA

Speaker's Dilemma is an elocution competition for those who can enthral the audience with their speaking skills, pronunciation, articulation, inflections and gestures.Bewitch the judges and the audience as you recite an original piece or one written by famous personalities.The rules of the event are:
1. Each participant will be allowed 5 minutes for speaking.
2. Languages permitted are English and Hindi.
3. Obscene content is not acceptable and will be disqualified immediately.
4. The decisions of the judges will be final and binding on all candidates.


SHERLOCKED

There is nothing more deceptive than an obvious fact. Sherlocked, an event where the quizzer in you gets a chance to flaunt and where you put your thinking cap to work. When in class, you put your brain aside, here you will get a chance to use it to long connect and gamble with literature.In Round one, each team will be quizzed on various topics related to literature, movies, comics and Mythology.The teams moving forward in the next round will get a chance to pounce till they bounce and gamble their heart and points away.PS: A very interesting Sherlock dedicated round awaits for you!Rules:
1) Team size is 2.
2) The event will consist of 2 rounds.
3) 6 teams will qualify for the final round.
4) Quiz master's decision is binding and final.


NEWSROOM

The glorious portrayal of chaos and mayhem by today's media houses has become tiresome and upsetting. Newsroom is where you bring in your best interactive and creative input while sustaining the spirit and essence of journalism. Arnab’s constant yelling and similar hullabaloo in other mainstream media platforms is not the need of the nation. The real challenge is to maintain the true and ideal scenario amidst all the uproar.Rules and procedure:
1) Team size is 2.
2) Round 1- Simulation of a newsroom.
3) Round 2- Debate and discussion.
4) Decision of judges is final and binding.


NEHLE PE DEHLA

Nehle pe Dehla is about your ability to think off the feet. You have to bring quick sequences of ideas to hit your opponent where it hurts. Yes! hit a dehla over that nehla. Nehle pe dehla is a Hindi extempore event. The participants are expected to extrapolate on the given topic. The topics might range from recent advancements in technology to the dynamics of world politics. It will test not only the knowledge but also presence of mind of the participants.Rules:
1. Team Size-1
2. Time- 2.5 minutes
3. Obscene language or reference in any form is not allowed.
4. Decision of the judges will be final and binding.


LIT POKER

Are you into playing roluettes and is reading novels your hobby , this is just the place for you. Where you can come flaunt your myraid of knowledge.Just pick up the right clue and be the winner of Litpoker.because this poker is not about playing cards it's about your love for literature.Rules are as follows:
1)the event will be conducted in two rounds
1st round:A number of clues will be provided (both right and wrong) , just pick up the right one and get into our round two.
2nd round:
.)6 finalists will be selected
.)a poker will be played based on literary works(no previous knowledge of poker is needed)
.) participants need to participate individually.


WHOSE LINE IS IT ANYWAY?

Can you be a first-rate broker who can sell ‘the rabbit pearl’ even to a nun or a glass of water to a drowning guy? Do you have the potential to win over the crowd with you striking replies? Can you be anything when the situation demands?If you are as awesome as you think you are then this is the stage to prove it. Partake and witness the beguiling game show sensation ever and act your way out to the top. Cause even William said it my friend that “All the world’s a stage, and all the men and women merely players”.
Rules:

2) Any kind of vulgarity is not allowed.
