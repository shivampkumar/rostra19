Rostra Registration

ROSTRA is East India's Largest Literary Fest conducted by the Literary Society, BIT Mesra in Birla Institute Of Technology, Mesra, Ranchi. The core of the festival resides in bringing out the various forms of literature and showcasing its soul to the most veracious essence. It is a platform for literature enthusiasts, writers, orators, artists and all those who bear the idea of doing something great. ROSTRA is a three-day fest filled with enthralling events, interactive sessions and fun games. The three days-Primum, Medius and Ultimus bring exciting events and performances from famous and recognized personalities while the nights are full of enticing performances.
The fest witnesses participants from BIT and all its branches, NIT’s, IIT’s, MNIT, KIIT, and different colleges in Ranchi, Dhanbad, Jamshedpur, Kolkata and other colleges all over Jharkhand. Team ROSTRA also invites students from different schools in Ranchi to take part in the fest. ROSTRA witnessed a footfall of 3000 last year and we aim to make ROSTRA great again.

Youth Parliament Registration

Youth Parliament
Democracy, apart from being a display of the power of the people, is also one of their virtues. The ability to take decisions for the greater good, and have faith in them. The Parliament of India is the cornerstone of democracy in the country.  So come and step into the shoes of the legislators, and join us in the simulation of the “Westminster” model of Government.
RULES
    1. Both single and double representation is allowed.
    2. The session of parliament will take place for 2 days.
    3. Participants will be provided with their respective portfolios, which they have to emulate.
    4. After the opening address by the honourable President, the Speaker will continue with the proceedings of the house, which shall include the Question hour, Debate and Discussion, among other matters. You shall be a representative of the portfolio allotted, and that should reflect in your stances in the house.
    5. At the end of the session, THE HOUSE WILL STAND ADJOURNED SINE DIE.
    6. Honourable members are advised to practice parliamentary politics with immense patience, decency and courtesy.
The organising team has all the rights to make any changes in the above mentioned rules.

Events

Panchayati Raj

Where calm means nothing and chaos is key – Panchayati Raj is the platform for you to embrace your rustic, rural roots and fight for what is yours. No quiet courtrooms or tedious settlement meetings; leave logic behind and simply convince the Panch to claim what is rightfully yours. And remember, the entire village will witness this spectacle, be careful not to lose your turban.

Rules for the event as follows:
1)No obscene language is allowed
2)Debating in Regional language is allowed. (Languages like Marwadi, bhojpuri, rajasthani)
3)Please report half an hour before the event starts.
4) Time limit and other details will be provided on spot.
5) Team Size : 3

Literati Combat

Fandom is akin to passion in more ways than one may fathom. How far will you go to prove your admiration for remarkably fine works literature? How hungrily does your core burn with passion for your favourite works of fiction? This is also an opportunity to let your imagination soar higher that is ever has before. Dig through your favourite comics and fairytales, the worlds that you witnessed as a naïve kid and the beasts that once scared the life out of you. Delve into an ocean of possibilities and emerge a storyteller.

Rules:

1) Team size: 2.

2) The event will be conducted in two Rounds.

Speaker's Dilemma

Eloquence is an uncommon yet extraordinary skill, a mastery of speaking that sways an audience of any capacity. Take the stage and prove your prowess in mesmerizing the masses with weapons far mightier than any other – the spoken word. Present an original, influenced or another author’s work and showcase your mastery at diction and enunciated, gripping speech.

Rules:

1. Each participant will be allowed 5 minutes for speaking.

2. Languages permitted are English and Hindi.

3. Obscene content is not acceptable and will be disqualified immediately.

4. The decisions of the judges will be final and binding on all candidates.


Sherlocked

There is nothing more deceptive than an obvious fact. If you possess a keen eye for fine details in works of literature that have revolutionized generations, it is time to let loose the literary sleuth in you. Thumb through all the books you can, memorize every name and place. Only the quick-witted will crack the rewards that the riddles hold.

1) Team size is 2.

2) The event will consist of 2 rounds.

3) 6 teams will qualify for the final round.

4) Quiz master's decision is binding and final.


Newsroom

How long can you resist yelling back from behind the news desk while debating? How will you maintain a straight face when the teleprompter goes bonkers and begins spewing nonsensical statements? If technical difficulties won’t bother your charisma and composure, we invite you to host our news hour.

Rules:

1) Team size is 2.

2) Round 1- Simulation of a newsroom.

3) Round 2- Debate and discussion.

4) Decision of judges is final and binding.


Nehle Pe Dehla

If you happen to know everything about anything, then this is your chance to exhibit your expansive expertise. From insane technological advances to mind-boggling conspiracy theories and twisted politics, we have you covered! Satiate your hunger all things unconventionally conversational and own your narrative.

Rules:

1. Team Size-1

2. Time- 2.5 minutes

3. Obscene language or reference in any form is not allowed.

4. Decision of the judges will be final and binding.


Lit Poker

Juggling the odds at a poker table could be fatal. Everyone watches with their hearts in their mouths as the tables turn. How high can the stakes be when your favourite book is on the betting table? If your literary knowledge and prowess are worthy betting on, place your bets and gamble your way to the prize. Let the deck decide whose favour the odds are in.

Rules:

1)the event will be conducted in two rounds

1st round: A number of clues will be provided (both right and wrong) , just pick up the right one and get into our round two.

2nd round:

->6 finalists will be selected

->a poker will be played based on literary works(no previous knowledge of poker is needed)

-> participants need to participate individually.


Whose line is it anyway?

Can you be a first-rate broker who can sell ‘the rabbit pearl’ even to a nun or a glass of water to a drowning guy? Do you have the potential to win over the crowd with you striking replies? If you can be anyone and anything when you feel like then this is the stage to prove it. Partake and witness the beguiling game show sensation and improv your way to the top.

Rules:

1) Event requires individual participation.

2) Any kind of vulgarity is not allowed.

About Us

Literary Society
Birla Institute Of Technology Mesra

Since more than 60 years, Birla Institute of Technology has been one of the most honoured and coveted educational institutions of the country. Standing majestically tall on a campus of 750 acres amidst luscious foliage, the propagation of knowledge here has been beyond compare.
Literary Society, BIT Mesra has always believed in crafting a platform to bring together writers, orators, artists and even those who just believe in liberal, unique ideas and making an impact. With our glorious past behind us, we aren't afraid to experiment, feeding the creativity that lies within. In East India's most honoured and coveted technical institute BIT Mesra, the Literary Society is the living testimony of the diverse talents and versatile personalities among us where we ensure to "keep the spark lit" with a hoping to create a sea of flames. Rostra is one of our humble attempts to unite creative minds and showcase the pure aura of literature.
